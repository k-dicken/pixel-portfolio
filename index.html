Kylee Dicken
a pixel artist

Pixel art has been my primary medium of art for around 9 years now. While studying programming I was fortunate enough to have been and be the lead artist on two different game projects, a teaching game for the Crane Naval base working with two other pixel artists and a recruitment game for IU Indianapolis' admissions website planned to launch in Spring of 2024. In my free time I have also provided merch for various fan zines/projects including the Type/cast zine which raised $520 for the Ukraine Crisis Relief Fund and the Vocaloid Hall of Fame zine which raised $844 for Stop AAPI Hate.
